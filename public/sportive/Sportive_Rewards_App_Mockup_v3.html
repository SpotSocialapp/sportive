Why this works

The app connects the things Sportive already has - locations, brands, categories, sizes, and ecommerce - into a loyalty engine that can be measured.

01

Personalized fit

Save sizes by customer and family member, then use profile data to push only relevant drops, replacements, and back-in-stock alerts.

02

Brand-led campaigns

Make Nike, On, adidas, New Balance, Jordan, and other key brands usable as segmented campaign themes instead of broad site banners.

03

Store traffic

Drive members to Bayside, CocoWalk, Dadeland, Dolphin Mall, and Sawgrass with geofenced events, location-specific offers, and QR redemption.
